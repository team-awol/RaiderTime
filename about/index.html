FAQ's

Signing up for your desired classroom in advance helps prevent overcrowding and helps organize our weekly raider time.

To sign up for a club meeting you need to sign up for the teacher's classroom who sponsors the club. I.e NHS members would sign up for William Reinhart's classroom.

Any teacher who is not available for that Raider Time will not show up on the drop-down list. If you think we are missing a teacher, please let us know by emailing us at atholtonADS@gmail.com.

If you have a question not answered on this page or are experiencing a bug with our website, please contact us at atholtonADS@gmail.com. We will get back to you as soon as possible!
